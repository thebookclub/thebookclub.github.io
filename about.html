The Book Club

We try our best to provide you with the ebooks of the novels you love! We can do this because our staff puts in time to scour through the ocean of the internet and find these beautiful novels so that they can make it available to you free of cost. Support us by following us on social media, donating to our cause be it in the form of time or money and do give us your much-valued feedback!

Book Formats

We're trying to provide you with the best quality ebooks we can find. This means that some books may be in different format than others. We curate the best novels by reading through all the books and selecting those which provide the best tradeoff between easiness of reading, layout of the book, logical structuring of chapters inbuilt in the book etc.
